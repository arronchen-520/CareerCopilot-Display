Privacy Policy for CareerCopilot

Last updated: February 17, 2026

1. Information We Collect

When you use CareerCopilot, we only request access to your basic Google profile information (such as Email address, Name, and User ID) through Google OAuth. We do not access your contacts, files, or any other sensitive data.

2. How We Use Your Information

Your email address is used solely for the following purposes:

  • To create and manage your personal account.
  • To allow you to save and retrieve your resumes and job-resume match scores.
  • To provide a personalized dashboard for your job hunting history.

3. Data Storage and Security

All data is securely stored in our encrypted Supabase database. We follow industry best practices to protect your information and will never sell, rent, or share your personal data with third parties.

4. Data Deletion

You have the right to request the deletion of your data at any time. If you wish to delete your account and all associated records, please contact us at the email address provided below.
